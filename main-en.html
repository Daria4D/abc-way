Your Achievements

Listening Comprehension

Our students actively immerse themselves in an English-speaking environment, listening to live speech from native speakers and completing tasks that develop their comprehension skills. This helps them understand spoken language and respond confidently in various situations.

Communication Skills

We create engaging lessons with interesting themes and projects that foster active interaction among students. Children learn to overcome language barriers, express their ideas, and engage in dialogue with both teachers and peers, building their communication confidence.

Reading

By using specially designed learning materials, students enhance their reading and text analysis skills. They learn to identify main ideas, summarize content, and understand unfamiliar words in context, deepening their comprehension of language and literature.

Writing

In class, students learn to write various types of texts, including narratives, descriptions, arguments, essays, and letters. We support a creative approach, allowing each child to find their unique style and voice in writing.
